About Me

I'm Vanshika Koshti I’m a passionate HTML Developer with a strong foundation in building clean, responsive, and accessible web pages. I specialize in writing semantic HTML5 code that ensures websites are both user-friendly and search-engine optimized.

With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of web standards, I bring designs to life with precision and performance in mind. I enjoy collaborating with designers, front-end developers, and teams to turn static visuals into interactive, browser-compatible experiences.

Whether it’s crafting pixel-perfect layouts or ensuring cross-browser consistency, I focus on delivering high-quality markup that forms the solid base for any web project.
